POLICY 4: EXPENSE AND TRAVEL 4.1 Business Expense Reimbursement Employees may be reimbursed for approved business expenses including meals, transportation, lodging, and supplies. Receipts are required for all expenses over $25. Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of incurrence and approved by direct manager before payment. 4.2 Travel Policy Business travel must be pre-approved and booked through our corporate travel agency.
