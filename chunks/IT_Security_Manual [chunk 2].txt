CHAPTER 2: ACCESS CONTROL 2.1 User Account Management User accounts are provisioned based on job role and business need. Access follows the principle of least privilege - users receive minimum access necessary to perform their duties. Account reviews are conducted quarterly. Terminated employee access is revoked immediately upon HR notification. 2.2 Multi-Factor Authentication MFA is required for all systems containing sensitive data including email, VPN, cloud applications, and administrative interfaces.
