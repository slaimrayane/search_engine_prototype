Approved MFA methods include authenticator apps, hardware tokens, and SMS (for low-risk applications only). 2.3 Password Requirements Passwords must be at least 12 characters with complexity requirements. Passphrases are encouraged over complex passwords. Password managers are provided to all employees and are required for storing work-related credentials.
